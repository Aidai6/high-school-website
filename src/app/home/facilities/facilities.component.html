Students Life

Our school offers out-of-timetable sessions that enrich and broaden the curriculum.
They include STEM Clubs, Sports, and Arts.

STEM Clubs

The STEM Club welcomes all WCC students interested in fields of science, technology, engineering and math in less formal settings.

Physical Activities

Our school has to offer sport activities on our well-equipped tennis, volleyball, football, and basketball playing grounds.

Music and Arts

Our students are exposed to musical education as well as to creative arts. Students engage in piano, komuz, vocal singing classes and take part in art exhibitions.
