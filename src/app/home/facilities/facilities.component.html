Students Life

Life in lyceum is busy, girls have all the opportunities to develop their hobbies and spend time with fun and productivity.
We encourage excellence in music, dance, sports and etc.

STEM Clubs

Our STEM Clubs are out-of-timetable sessions that enrich and broaden the curriculum, giving our students the chance to explore subjects like science, technology, engineering and maths in less formal settings.

Physical Activities

We strongly believe that physical activities not only benefit their bodies and physiological performances but also their mental conditions as well as improving their cognitive performances. Therefore, our school has to offer sport activities on our well-equipped tennis, volleyball, football, and basketball playing grounds.

Music and Arts

Our students are exposed to musical education as well as to creative arts. We believe that playing musical instruments, drawing and painting lets our students explore and expose their creative potentials. Therefore, our school provides piano, komuz, vocal singing classes and organizes art exhibitions.
